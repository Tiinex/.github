# Kickstarter Rewards — v1

## Reward Philosophy

Tiinex rewards are intentionally lightweight, transparent, and continuity-oriented.

Backers are supporting:
- continued public infrastructure work
- recoverable continuity systems
- open experimentation
- observable development
- long-term ecosystem survivability

The goal is not exclusivity, artificial scarcity, or heavy fulfillment obligations.

The reward model is designed to:
- minimize admin overhead
- avoid creator burnout
- preserve operational continuity
- keep the ecosystem public and inspectable



# Identity & Credits

Backers may choose one of the following visibility modes:

| Mode | Meaning |
|------|---------|
| Named | Publicly acknowledged in supporter lineage |
| [REDACTED] | Contribution acknowledged without identity |

No hidden supporter fingerprinting or persistent anonymous tracking is used.

If identity is omitted, the continuity remains visible while the identity is intentionally removed.

Example:

```md
supporters:
- Sigma
- Leo
- [REDACTED]
````
