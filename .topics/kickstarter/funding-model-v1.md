# Kickstarter Funding Model v1

## Funding Philosophy

The Kickstarter is designed around sustainable continuity rather than maximum extraction.

The goal is not:
- explosive startup scaling
- artificial growth pressure
- unsustainable delivery velocity

The goal is creating stable operational conditions for continued public continuity work.

---

# Core Campaign Goal

## Proposed Initial Goal
200 000 SEK

### Why

This creates:
- operational runway
- infrastructure stability
- sustainability margin
- recoverable pacing

without requiring unrealistic delivery pressure.

---

# Sustainment Model

## Monthly Sustainment Target
50 000 SEK gross / month

### Why

This reflects:
- prior real-world salary baseline
- sustainable full-time engineering work
- long-term operational continuity

The target is intentionally grounded and not positioned as founder extraction.

---

# Operational Reserve

## Minimum Reserve
1 month sustainment reserve

### Purpose

The reserve exists to:
- avoid panic-driven decisions
- preserve continuity under degraded states
- allow time for operational transitions
- avoid forcing unsustainable delivery behavior

This reserve is considered part of continuity sustainability.

The infrastructure pool may include:

# Bonus Pool Philosophy

The bonus pool is intentionally separated from operational necessity.

Purpose:
- appreciation
- recognition
- optional additional support
- continuity acceleration

Bonus funding should not automatically redefine:
- roadmap direction
- promises
- delivery guarantees

The goal is avoiding incentive distortion.

---

# Public Funding Visibility

Project-level withdrawals may be traced publicly through continuity artifacts.

Examples:
- sustainment withdrawals
- infrastructure expenses
- tooling purchases
- runtime costs

The goal is:
- operational observability
- recoverable project continuity
- inspectable funding lineage

Personal life outside explicit project funding remains private unless intentionally shared.

---

# Failure Handling Philosophy

If funding remains below ideal targets:
- scope may narrow
- cadence may slow
- priorities may shift

However:
- artifacts remain public
- continuity remains recoverable
- reasoning remains inspectable
- progress remains reconstructable

Partial continuity is still valuable continuity.

# Long-Term Direction

The long-term vision is not maximizing funding extraction.

- sustainable public continuity work
- portable collaboration
- recoverable workflows
