# Media Plan v1

## Purpose

The Kickstarter media strategy should:
- explain quickly
- build trust
- demonstrate reality
- avoid hype overload
- make continuity concepts visually understandable

---

## Core Media Targets

### Kickstarter Trailer

Goal:
introduce the philosophy and practical direction.

Focus:
- continuity
- recoverability
- inspectability
- lineage
- explicit workflows

---

### Visual Roadmaps

Goal:
help viewers understand the ecosystem shape quickly.

Potential visuals:
- continuity runtime diagrams
- lineage trees
- reconstruction flows
- topic structures
- export/import diagrams

---

### Public Trace Examples

Goal:
demonstrate operational reality.

Examples:
- development traces
- funding traces
- lineage evolution
- reconstruction workflows
- recoverability examples

---

### Screenshots And UI Concepts

Goal:
make abstract ideas feel concrete.

Potential assets:
- repository structures
- trace inspectors
- continuity timelines
- lineage visualizations
- topic navigation

---

## Streaming / Public Development

Potential direction:
- public development sessions
- continuity walkthroughs
- trace creation streams
- reconstruction demonstrations
- contributor onboarding examples

---

## Tone

The media strategy should avoid:
- fake futurism
- AGI hype
- artificial scarcity
- exaggerated claims

Preferred tone:
- grounded
- operational
- transparent
- exploratory
- reality-first

---

## Long-Term Goal

The long-term goal is making:
recoverable continuity feel understandable, practical, and human.
