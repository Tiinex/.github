# Kickstarter Campaign Structure — v1

## Purpose

This document defines the intended structural flow of the Tiinex Kickstarter campaign.

The goal is not simply to explain technology.

The goal is to:
- establish reality contact
- explain the continuity problem
- present Tiinex as a recoverability-oriented infrastructure experiment
- communicate operational philosophy
- explain sustainability needs
- invite participation in public continuity work

The structure intentionally prioritizes:
- clarity over hype
- recoverability over perfection
- operational realism over marketing abstraction

---

# 1. Immediate Hook

## Goal

Create immediate recognition that continuity loss is a real problem.

## Focus

People should quickly recognize:
- AI workflows drift
- chats collapse
- reasoning disappears
- context gets trapped inside runtimes
- reconstruction becomes difficult after the fact

## Tone

Grounded.
Simple.
Direct.

Avoid:
- buzzwords
- futurism overload
- platform claims
- “AI revolution” language

---
