# Tiinex — Infrastructure for Owned Continuity

## The Problem

AI-assisted workflows are becoming increasingly powerful, but continuity between sessions, tools, runtimes, and collaborators is still fragile.

Context disappears.
Reasoning becomes opaque.
Artifacts lose provenance.
Collaboration drifts across platforms and time.

Most systems optimize for convenience.

Very few optimize for recoverability.

---

## Why Continuity Matters

Human collaboration is rarely linear.

Ideas evolve.
Interpretations drift.
Artifacts change.
Projects degrade and recover over time.

In practice, the important question is often not:

> “Was this perfectly true?”

But rather:

> “Can we reconstruct how we got here?”

Tiinex explores tooling and workflows designed around recoverable continuity instead of hidden state and opaque memory.

---

## What Tiinex Is

Tiinex is an open continuity infrastructure project focused on:

- explicit traces
- recoverable lineage
- portable workflows
- inspectable artifacts
- runtime-agnostic continuity

The ecosystem currently explores:

- `.trace.md`
- lineage chains
- continuity carry-forward
- markdown-first workflows
- portable exports
- recoverable state reconstruction

The goal is not replacing humans.

The goal is helping humans and AI systems collaborate more transparently across time.
