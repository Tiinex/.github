# Kickstarter Structure v1

## Campaign Duration

### Proposed Length
67 days

### Why

The campaign is intentionally longer than a traditional short-form hype cycle.

Tiinex explores:
- continuity over time
- recoverable iteration
- observable evolution
- public collaboration drift
- sustained operational workflows

A longer campaign window allows:
- slower organic discovery
- artifact accumulation
- public traces
- continuity observation
- iterative clarification
- recoverable discussion history

The goal is not maximizing launch-day hype velocity.

The goal is testing whether continuity can remain meaningful across time.

---

# Campaign Philosophy

The Kickstarter is not presented as:
- a finished product
- a magical AI system
- a guaranteed future platform

It is presented as:
- an open continuity infrastructure initiative
- a public operational experiment
- an evolving artifact ecosystem
- a recoverable collaboration model

---

# Funding Pools

## 1. Salary Pool

Purpose:
Operational sustainment for continued full-time work on Tiinex.

This represents:
- development time
- research
- documentation
- ecosystem coordination
- public continuity work

### Proposed Baseline
Based on prior real-world salary level.

The goal is operational sustainability rather than hyper-growth extraction.

---

## 2. Token Pool

Purpose:
Runtime and infrastructure costs.

Examples:
- AI inference
- API usage
- hosting
- tooling
- exports
- processing
- experimentation

This pool helps sustain active development workflows.

---

## 3. Bonus Pool

Purpose:
Optional appreciation-based support.

This pool is intentionally separated from operational necessity.

It represents:
- appreciation
- recognition
- optional extra support
- continuity acceleration

Bonus funding does not redefine project direction automatically.

---

# Withdrawal Philosophy

Operational withdrawals from project funding should be traceable at the project level.

Examples:
- salary withdrawals
- tooling expenses
- infrastructure costs
- project purchases

The goal is:
- operational observability
- continuity reconstruction
- funding lineage visibility

Personal life outside explicit project funding remains private unless intentionally shared.

Tiinex does not aim to create forced-total-transparency culture.

---

# Stretch Expansion Philosophy

Additional funding expands:
- operational runway
- tooling quality
- interoperability
- viewers
- adapters
- documentation
- recoverability tooling

Stretch goals should remain:
- realistic
- recoverable
- operationally grounded

Funding should not force artificial roadmap inflation.

---

# Campaign Cadence

The campaign should remain visibly active throughout its duration.

Possible cadence:
- weekly updates
- artifact releases
- trace examples
- continuity reflections
- technical experiments
- reconstruction examples
- ecosystem observations

The goal is continuity movement rather than constant hype escalation.

---

# Sustainability Philosophy

Tiinex is intentionally developed around sustainable continuity rather than burnout-driven velocity.

The project values:
- long-term operational stability
- recoverable progress
- observable evolution
- human sustainability

Development continues during the campaign, but not through unsustainable crunch behavior.

---

# Success Criteria

Success is not only:
- funding amount
- attention spikes
- social metrics

Success also includes:
- continuity formation
- meaningful public understanding
- artifact adoption
- recoverable collaboration
- ecosystem resonance
- long-term sustainability

---

# Failure Philosophy

Even partial outcomes remain valuable if:
- artifacts survive
- reasoning remains reconstructable
- continuity remains inspectable
- lessons remain recoverable

Tiinex treats degraded and partial states as valid operational reality rather than hidden failure states.
