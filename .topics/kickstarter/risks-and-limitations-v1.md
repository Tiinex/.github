# Risks And Limitations v1

## This Project Is Experimental

Tiinex is exploratory infrastructure work.

Many ideas are still evolving and should be treated as experimental rather than finalized standards.

---

## Adoption Risk

The ecosystem introduces workflows that may initially feel unfamiliar.

Adoption may remain limited unless:
- tooling improves
- documentation improves
- workflows become easier to understand

---

## Runtime Fragmentation

Different tools and runtimes behave differently.

One challenge is preserving continuity across:
- platforms
- AI systems
- editors
- workflows
- automation layers

---

## Metadata Drift

Lineage systems can accumulate:
- conflicting interpretations
- incomplete traces
- missing references
- partial reconstruction states

The project intentionally treats uncertainty as first-class instead of hiding it.

---

## Maintenance Burden

Explicit continuity systems can create additional maintenance work.

Part of the research focuses on reducing unnecessary operational overhead.

---

## Governance Complexity

Human collaboration introduces:
- disagreements
- interpretation conflicts
- trust issues
- moderation problems
- social scaling challenges

No technical system fully removes these realities.

---

## Funding Uncertainty

The Kickstarter helps support development time, but long-term sustainability is not guaranteed.

The project intentionally avoids pretending future success is guaranteed.

---

## Operational Philosophy

The project prioritizes:
- recoverability over perfection
- inspectability over opacity
- explicit limitations over false certainty
