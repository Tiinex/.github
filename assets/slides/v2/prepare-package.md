# PREPARE PACKAGE — TIINEX VISUAL SYSTEM V2

## Purpose

Preparation work required before generating new TIINEX scenes.

Goals:
- reduce AI-generated feeling
- improve continuity
- improve realism
- separate content from template
- support emotional coherence

---

## 1. CHARACTER BIBLE

Create reusable character sheets.

Define:
- role
- clothing
- posture
- emotional baseline
- recurring objects
- interaction style

Characters must remain visually recognizable between scenes.

---

## 2. ENVIRONMENT BIBLE

Reuse recurring locations:
- meeting rooms
- support desks
- collaboration spaces
- operations rooms
- lounge areas

Scenes should feel like the SAME organization over time.

---

## 3. TIME CONTINUITY

Suggested progression:
- late afternoon
- evening
- late evening
- calm night
- reflective ending

Subtle environmental continuity matters.

---

## 4. CAMERA LANGUAGE

Rules:
- documentary realism
- restrained cinematics
- eye-level framing
- no hero shots
- no sci-fi spectacle framing

---

## 5. COLOR SCRIPT

Examples:
- colder tones during drift
- neutral realism during friction
- slightly warmer tones during recovery

Avoid neon overload.

---

## 6. TEMPLATE SEPARATION

Separate:
- content layer
- presentation layer

Presentation layer:
- logo
- footer
- slide numbers
- side panels
- overlays

Do NOT generate both together initially.

---

## 7. GRID SYSTEM

Define:
- safe zones
- text zones
- focal regions
- footer spacing
- logo spacing

Every slide should feel structurally related.

---

## 8. TEXT DENSITY RULES

Hard limits:
- one primary idea per slide
- minimal supporting text
- avoid paragraph density

Target:
40–60% less text than V1.

---

## 9. OBJECT CONTINUITY

Reuse recurring props:
- mugs
- notebooks
- lamps
- laptops
- chairs
- whiteboards

Creates subconscious continuity.

---

## 10. VIEWER POSITION

Viewer should feel like:
- quiet observer
- inside the organization
- witnessing coordination and drift

Avoid detached marketing energy.

---

## 11. ANTI-RULES

Never generate:
- holograms
- floating interfaces
- cyberpunk cities
- unrealistic control rooms
- excessive post-it clutter

Goal:
Grounded operational realism.

---

## 12. GENERATION PIPELINE

Step 1:
Generate environments.

Step 2:
Generate recurring characters.

Step 3:
Compose scenes.

Step 4:
Apply template overlays separately.

---

## FINAL PRINCIPLE

Optimize for:
- recognition
- realism
- trust
- coherence
- recoverability
- emotional clarity
